How MPDDMS Works

Welcome to the Maize Plant Disease Detection and Management System (MPDDMS). This page provides an overview of how to use our platform:

  1. Upload Image: On the Upload Image page, upload an image of your maize crop with visible disease symptoms.
  2. View Results: After uploading the image, click the "Upload" button. You will be redirected to the Results page where you can view the detected diseases.
  3. Chatbot Interaction: On the Results page, click the "Chat with Chatbot" button to interact with the chatbot. The chatbot provides personalized management strategies for the detected diseases.
  4. Provide Feedback: After interacting with the chatbot, click the "Feedback" button to provide your feedback and suggestions for improvement.
